Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our GATE coaching programs

What is the duration of the GATE coaching programs?

Regular Classroom Program: 12 months
Weekend Batch Program: 8 months
Crash Course Program: 3 months

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, all enrolled students receive comprehensive study materials including theory books, practice question banks, previous year papers, and access to our online test series.

What is the batch size?

We maintain small batch sizes (maximum 30 students per batch) to ensure personalized attention and better interaction between faculty and students.

Is there any scholarship available?

Yes, we offer scholarships based on academic merit and financial need. GATE toppers and students with excellent academic records can avail up to 50% scholarship.

Can I get a refund if I change my mind?

We offer a 7-day refund policy. If you're not satisfied with our teaching methodology or course content within the first week, you can request a full refund.

Do you provide hostel facilities?

We have tie-ups with nearby hostels and PG accommodations. Our counseling team can help you find suitable accommodation options in Delhi.
