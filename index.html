About GATE Examination

Everything you need to know about GATE and why it's crucial for your career

What is GATE?

GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) is a national-level entrance exam for admission to M.Tech/Ph.D. programs in IITs, NITs, and other prestigious institutions. It's also the gateway to lucrative careers in PSUs.

Exam Pattern

Computer-based test with 65 questions (MCQs and NAT) worth 100 marks. Duration: 3 hours. Subjects include Engineering Mathematics, General Aptitude, and Technical subjects.

Career Opportunities

High GATE scores open doors to M.Tech admissions, PSU jobs (NTPC, BHEL, IOCL, etc.), research positions, and international opportunities with attractive salaries.
